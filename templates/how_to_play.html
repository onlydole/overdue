How to Play

Everything you need to know about managing the library.

🎮 The Game Loop

📖
1. Shelve

Create volumes of knowledge and organize them on shelves.

⏰
2. Wait

Over time, volumes gather dust. Their Dewey Score decays.

🔍
3. Review

Review volumes to reset their Dewey Score back to 100.

⭐
4. Level Up

Earn XP, unlock badges, and climb the leaderboard.

📊 The Dewey Score

Every volume has a freshness score from 0 to 100. It starts at 100 when created or reviewed, then decays over time -- like books gathering dust.

75+
Pristine

Freshly reviewed, perfectly current. The gold standard.

50-74
Good

Slightly dusty, still reliable. A nudge wouldn't hurt.

25-49
Dusty

Getting stale. This knowledge needs attention soon.

0-24
Overdue

Significantly outdated. Review immediately or risk losing it!

📖 Pages Read (XP)

Earn "pages read" by keeping the library healthy.

Shelve a new volume +10 pages

Review a current volume +5 pages
Daily streak bonus +15 pages/day
All shelf volumes above 75 +50 pages

🏢 Reading Room Mood

The library's overall health is reflected in the Reading Room's mood. Keep your volumes fresh to maintain a calm atmosphere.

📖
Quiet Study
Average Dewey 80-100. Warm golden light, everything is serene.
📚
Gentle Hum
Average Dewey 60-79. Normal lighting, a slight bustle in the air.
📘
Getting Noisy
Average Dewey 40-59. Flickering lights, books slightly askew.
🔥
Call for Order
Average Dewey 20-39. Red accents, warning indicators everywhere.
🚧
Closed for Renovation
Average Dewey 0-19. The library is in crisis. Review everything!
