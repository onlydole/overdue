Your Knowledge Is Fading

Every day, what you've learned gathers dust -- facts blur, details slip away, connections break. Overdue is a game about taking knowledge back.

{{ render_icon('books', 18, '#f0c543') }} The Library

You are a librarian. Your volumes are your knowledge -- every fact, idea, and insight you want to keep. Organize them on shelves. Each volume has a Dewey Score that measures how fresh it is.

Left alone, knowledge decays. Dust settles. Scores drop. Your job is to keep the stacks pristine.

{{ render_icon('gamepad', 18, '#f0c543') }} The Game Loop

{{ render_icon('book-open', 24) }}
1. Shelve

Capture what you know before it escapes. Create volumes and organize them on shelves.

{{ render_icon('clock', 24) }}
2. Wait

Time is the enemy. Dust settles on every volume. Dewey Scores decay hour by hour.

{{ render_icon('search', 24) }}
3. Review

Brush off the dust. Review a volume to reset its Dewey Score back to 100.

{{ render_icon('star', 24) }}
4. Level Up

The more you maintain, the higher you climb. Earn XP, unlock badges, rise in rank.

{{ render_icon('fire', 18, '#f0c543') }} Why Play?

Your brain forgets. Overdue makes remembering a game.

Build a habit of revisiting what matters.

Compete with friends (or bots) on the leaderboard.

Watch your library thrive -- or fall into chaos.

{{ render_icon('chart', 18, '#f0c543') }} The Dewey Score

Every volume has a freshness score from 0 to 100. It starts at 100 when created or reviewed, then decays over time -- like books gathering dust on a neglected shelf.

75+
Pristine

Freshly reviewed, perfectly current. The gold standard.

50-74
Good

Slightly dusty, still reliable. A nudge wouldn't hurt.

25-49
Dusty

Getting stale. This knowledge needs attention soon.

0-24
Overdue

Significantly outdated. Review immediately or risk losing it!

{{ render_icon('book-open', 18, '#f0c543') }} Pages Read (XP)

Earn "pages read" by keeping the library healthy.

Shelve a new volume +10 XP (10 pages)
Review an overdue volume +10 XP (10 pages, 2x)
Rescue bonus (for overdue review) +20 XP (20 pages)
Review a current volume +5 XP (5 pages)
Daily streak bonus +15 XP/day (15 pages)
All shelf volumes above 75 +50 XP (50 pages)
